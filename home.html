Oceans 32 Guesthouse




A warm welcome awaits all guests at Oceans 32 Guesthouse, a new guesthouse in East London, overlooking a pristine part of the Indian Ocean.

Monkeys, and birdlife are viewed on a daily basis, along with whale watching and buck on the rarer occasions. We are situated in a quiet area at the end of a cul de sac, allowing traffic to be a sound only to be remembered.

We have 5 bedrooms, all tastefully decorated with crisp white linen. The house offers flat screen tv’s, dstv, 3 braai areas, an entertainment area with darts, a pool table, bar area, a solar heated indoor swimming pool and much more.

We are the perfect spot for both business and leisure travellers, offering a tranquil atmosphere conducive to rest and rejuvenation and a short distance away from shopping centres, nightlife, etc. for those requiring something busier.
